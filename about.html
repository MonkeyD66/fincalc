Why We Built FinCalc Pro

The Problem We Solved

Financial calculators existed, but they were either outdated, inaccurate, intrusive (asking for personal data), or behind paywalls. We decided to build something different — something professionals would be proud of and everyone could use freely.

Our team includes financial analysts, software engineers, and designers who believe financial tools should be:

  • ✅ Accurate — Built with proper financial formulas and tested rigorously
  • ✅ Private — No data collection, no tracking, no storage
  • ✅ Beautiful — Modern design that works on any device
  • ✅ Free — Forever free, no paywalls, no "premium" versions
  • ✅ Global — Supporting multiple currencies and financial systems
